📋 Data Sources & Methodology
🗺️ Elevation Data
SRTM 30m DEM from NASA/USGS — 36×36 real elevation grids per city, bilinear interpolated to 72×72 for rendering.
NASA SRTM USGS EarthExplorer
🌦️ Weather API
Live data from Open-Meteo (free, no key) — temperature, precipitation, wind speed at 15-min resolution for all 4 Taiwan cities.
Open-Meteo API CWA Taiwan
🔬 Physics Engine
Manning's equation for flow velocity · Saint-Venant shallow water equations · SCS Curve Number runoff model (CN 60–98) · Horton infiltration · Penman-Monteith evapotranspiration.
Manning 1891 SCS-CN Model Horton 1933
💰 Damage Model
National Disaster Prevention Bureau (NDPB) Taiwan methodology — residential, agricultural, infrastructure, and business disruption costs calibrated to 2009 Morakot and 2001 Nari historical records.
Taiwan NDPB Morakot Report 2009
👥 Population Data
2023 Taiwan Ministry of Interior census by district — 8 districts per city with real elevations for flood exposure calculation.
MOI Taiwan 2023 District Census
